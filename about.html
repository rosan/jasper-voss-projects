Japser Voss

Jasper Voss (b.1978) studied anthropology at the University of Heidelberg, followed by History of Art at The Courtauld Institute, London.
In 2008 he was made artistic director of the Faschingbauer-Schloss Collection, where he curated notable exhibitions ‘I’m Here and It’s Now’ and ‘P-P-P-Post’.
Voss left The F-S Collection in 2013 to become a freelance writer andto pursue Jasper Voss Projects.
